About Suppressed

Start off as a naive boy with ambitions to escape to another country. Experience your family getting exiled by authorities, innocents in prison, starving citizens, prostitution, and more. Can you manage to escape your nation?

Suppressed’s goal is to educate and entertain at the same time. Those who play will hopefully become more empathetic with victims of human rights abuses and aware of what is happening in North Korea.

  • Storyline on real world issue
  • Human rights themes
  • Painterly art and animations
  • 2 endings
